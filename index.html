DailyPlay

Your Personal Daily Music Playlist

Phones don't have the capacity for your entire library, and trying to choose a list of songs to keep on your device at all times can be difficult. Over time, those few songs become stale and you become bored of listening to them. Constantly having to change the songs is an unnecessary hassle.

DailyPlay is an Android application that downloads a new playlist of music everyday. It randomly chooses and downloads a number of songs, so that you get different music each day. Keep your commute music exciting, and never let your library get stale.

It's Your Music

You probably have a huge music library. Let's face it, it doesn't fit on your phone, but it probably fits on the cloud. DailyPlay downloads your songs from your Google Play Music Library, so the playlist you get is made from your own personal music collection.

No Data. No Problem!

DailyPlay downloads the music at night as to use your Wi-Fi, so that there are no unnecessary data charges. If you don't have access to data while travelling (RIP subway users), then even better. Since your songs are downloaded the night before, your new music is ready for your morning commute.

The Perfect Length

30 minute commute? Then download 30 minutes of new music everyday. Need 10 songs for while at the gym? Then download by the exact amount of music you need. The option is yours, but either way you get the perfect length playlist.
