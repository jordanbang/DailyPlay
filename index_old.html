DailyPlay

Your Personal Daily Music Playlist

Phones don't have the capacity for your entire library, and trying to choose a list of songs to keep on your device at all times can be difficult. Over time, those few songs become stale and you become bored of listening to them. Constantly having to change the songs is an uncessary hassle.

A new playlist every day means your commute music never gets old.

Download songs at night while at home on WiFi, so no unecessary data charges.

You set the limit, so you get the perfect length playlist.

Uses your Google Play Music Library to make your playlist personalized.

DailyPlay is your personal daily music playlist.

Check it out on the Google Play Store..
